UT Graduate ECE Student Organization
GREECE
GRaduatE ECE (GREECE) serves to foster a sense of community within the UT Graduate ECE department. To this end, we host social events, industry events, and provide a voice to the administration on behalf of students' needs.
We have tons of fun events planned for Spring 2019 school year, so stay in touch by adding yourself to the GREECE Slack and Email list!
