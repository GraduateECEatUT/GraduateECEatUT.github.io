UT Graduate ECE Student Organization
GREECE
The mission of GraduatE ECE (GREECE) is to interact with ECE graduate students across labs and tracks and foster a sense of community within the UT Graduate ECE department. We host social events, industry events, and provide a voice to the administration on behalf of students' needs.
We have tons of fun events planned for Fall 2018/Spring 2019 school year, so stay in touch!
